Sales & Recognition: A Quiet Climb

Though Rune Symbol hasn’t reached mainstream popularity, it has carved out a loyal niche following. It first launched quietly in late 2021 with modest sales. In 2024, after a limited print run sold out, sales saw a noticeable uptick.

Critics call it “a cerebral fantasy with surprising emotional depth.” Fans of Made in Abyss or The Ancient Magus’ Bride are especially drawn to its lore and aesthetic.
